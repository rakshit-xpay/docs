### Account Creation and KYB Onboarding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Head over to https://app.xpaycheckout.com/user-register to create your
    account
    <Tip>Make sure to sign up with your official email</Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Login">
    Once the account is created, login at https://app.xpaycheckout.com/login
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete KYB">
    You should see a `Complete KYB` button on your dashboard. Please go through
    the flow and submit documents to complete your flow.
    <Note>
      It typically takes upto 48 hours for your account to be verified and
      approved.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Account approval">
    On account approval, you should receive an email. You can then start
    collecting real world payments ðŸŽ‰
  </Step>
</Steps>
